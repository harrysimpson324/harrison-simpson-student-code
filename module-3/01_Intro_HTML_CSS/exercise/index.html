All About M. C. Escher

Some significant dates in the early life of M. C. Escher:

Date Event
June 17, 1898 Maurits Cornelis Escher was born in the Netherlands
Year of 1903 His family moved to a city called Arnhem located in the Netherlands
Year of 1918 He started attending school at the Technical College of Delft
1919 - 1922 He attended the Haarlem School of Architecture and Decorative Arts
Year of 1922 He traveled around Europe, visiting Italy and Spain. He became inspired by repetitive tile patterns seen during these travels, which can be seen in his work with tesselation

Quotes by M. C. Escher:

To have peace with this peculiar life; to accept what we do not understand; to wait calmly for what awaits us, you have to be wiser than I am.
We adore chaos because we love to produce order.
The things I want to express are so beautiful and pure.
